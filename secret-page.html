THE EDUCATORS HAVE BEEN LYING TO US

They haven't really saved us from a toxic wasteland by allowing us to live and work underground. The surface is beautiful and the air is clean!! We thought we were supporting the building of a domed city to live on the surface without fear of the toxic gases but it was all a lie. There are no workers up there suffering in the fumes, building the city so they can gain entry once its done. Really we're slaves to the rich living above working down here just so they can have cheap labor. They tell the people above that robots maintain the city and carry out their psychotic desires. BUT THATS US!! We'll never get out of the foundation. They'll keep us here forever!!
